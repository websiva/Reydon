UPS/Inverter battery

About the Product

UPS/Inverter system supplies conditioned utility power to its outlets and keeps its internal battery charged as long as the electricity is on. It also safeguards your data and valuable gadgets against power issues such as power surges and unexpected voltages. If the power goes out, the UPS/Inverter system provides backup power from its internal battery.

Your appliances are under constant threat from power problems. Even a brief loss of power or a surge can cause priceless data to be corrupted, ranging from corporate documents to family images. This can be avoided with the use of a UPS. UPS systems are simple to operate. Simply connect the UPS to a wall outlet, connect your equipment to the UPS outlets, and turn the UPS on.
