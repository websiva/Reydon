Pure Sine Wave Lithium UPS

ADIRATH LITHIUM UPS is the device that powers the electric appliances in the event of power failure. The PWM circuit generates a PURE SINEWAVE around 50Hz that super impose the AC generated by the inverter transformer. The DC voltage from the lithium battery is first converted into low volt AC by the DC-AC supply.

Features:
  • DSP based Pure Shine Wave UPS
  • 20 KHz Noiseless Operation
  • More Efficient & Human Error
  • LCD with Audible Alarm
  • Build-in Thermal Sensor
