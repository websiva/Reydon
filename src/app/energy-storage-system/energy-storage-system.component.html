Application

Battery energy storage systems have a varied range of applications. Commercial applications comprise of peak shaving, load shifting, emergency backup, and various grid services. Residential applications include self-consumption, off-grid homes, and crisis backup.

Energy storage is being increasingly examined for its potential to provide significant benefits. The ability to store energy provides a way to add flexibility to how power is produced and used, as well as a way to manage peak loads on the system by storing renewable electricity during low-demand periods. Energy storage technologies have the potential to provide ancillary services to the electricity grid, such as ensuring the dependability and stability of the power system and better matching generation to demand for electricity, in addition to supplying power on demand.
